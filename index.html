Our Services

Our clients include world's top universities and pharmaceutical organizations

Affinity Screen

A bead based peptide display engine, using FACS, allows for faster peptide discovery by binding affinity.

Functional Screen

The ability to display high peptide copy number allows discovery of functional peptides, such as agonists and antagonists, in primary screens

Targets

The KS-V platform is target agnostic giving access to cell surface receptors, notably GPCRs and ion channels. We have a dedicated team focused on production of targets to ensure quality of starting materials

Complex Screening

KS-V have developed a range of novel selection methodologies ranging from simple biopanning; more complex affinity screens; FACS based selection and sorting for functional screening; and single cell analysis.

Single Cell Analysis

Using expertise in cell line development and assay development, in conjunction with FACS, high content imaging and microfluidics, the Orbit platform can distinguish a functional response from single cells

High Throughput

The microfluidic capability allows for fine control over our library generation and for screening and manipulation of single cells in droplets.
